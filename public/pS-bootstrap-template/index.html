Our Design Goals.

✻

Let’s reclaim our interface with machines. Since the genesis of computers, one of the major question that has driven their evolution is - how would Humans interact with Computers?

It's all about the Questions.

Most of what we do today with computers is to get an Answer or to perform a Task. What better way to do this than ‘Ask someone’ - in the language we speak.

Make your NLU good at one thing.

It’s like your Conversational Assistant saying, ‘I am not a jack of all trades. I am good at one thing and I try my best to do understand that really, really well’.

Simplification.

Conversational Interfaces and related technologies need not be rocket science. How do we simplify Conversational Apps for enterprises?
